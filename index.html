HealthCare

Goal Of the Final app

According to the IDF Diabetes atlas 8 education,its estimated that 15.5 million adults aged 20-79 years were living with diabetes in the IDF African region in 2017,representing a regional prevalence of 3.3%.The highest prevalence is found in adults of age 55 to 65 years.The region has the highest proportion of undiagnosed diabetes, with two-third of adults living with diabetes unaware.More than half of these adults live in the urban areas.
This application is one that helps users keep track of there health conditions by enabling them monitor certain health conditions such as blood pressure,blood sugar, and calorie intake.Other useful features that this application will seek to provide is a medication reminder and helpful tips that will help the user handling blood sugar,blood pressure and calories should any of this conditions be on the high.
A primary persona of this application is Peter Okoye a 65 year old retiree in Nigeria currently diagnosed off diabetes who plans to monitor his sugar and calorie intake.Peter will be able to view and monitor the results on a daily,weekly and monthly basis.This will enable Peter set goals and monitor it.


Summary of goals:
  • Help users keep track of their health conditions such as blood sugar,pressure and calories over time
  • Remind them on taking there medication
  • Provide useful tips to users to enable them stay healthy
  • Provide a minimal interface for fast direct usage.
